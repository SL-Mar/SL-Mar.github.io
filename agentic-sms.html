Agentic SMS

The n8n of Marine QHSE - Workflow automation for ship management systems.

Project Overview

Agentic SMS reimagines ship management systems by applying workflow automation principles to maritime operations. Inspired by n8n's visual workflow approach, this system enables ship operators to design, automate, and monitor QHSE (Quality, Health, Safety, Environment) processes through AI-powered decision support.

Vision

Traditional ship management systems are rigid and process-heavy. Agentic SMS introduces a flexible, workflow-based approach where maritime operations are orchestrated through intelligent agents that understand maritime regulations, safety protocols, and operational constraints.

Key Concepts

Workflow Automation

Visual workflow design for QHSE processes, eliminating repetitive manual tasks.

AI Decision Support

Intelligent agents assist with compliance checks, risk assessment, and operational decisions.

QHSE Integration

Unified platform for Quality, Health, Safety, and Environment management.

Maritime Compliance

Built-in understanding of international maritime regulations and best practices.

Technology Stack

n8n Python Workflow Engine AI Agents Maritime QHSE

Current Status

The project is in the design and architecture phase. Focus areas include defining core workflow patterns for common maritime operations, establishing the agent communication framework, and mapping QHSE requirements to automated workflows.
