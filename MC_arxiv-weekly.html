Deep Learning for High-Frequency Trading: A Novel Approach to Market Prediction

Jane Doe, John Smith, Alice Johnson

We present a novel deep learning architecture for high-frequency trading that combines transformer-based attention mechanisms with reinforcement learning. Our approach achieves state-of-the-art performance on real-world trading data, demonstrating practical applications in financial markets. The system processes market data in real-time and makes trading decisions with minimal latency. We evaluate our method on multiple asset classes including equities, futures, and cryptocurrencies, showing consistent performance improvements over traditional approaches.
