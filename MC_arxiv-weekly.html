Deep Learning for Short Term Equity Trend Forecasting: A Behavior Driven Multi Factor Approach

Li et al.

This paper presents a novel deep learning architecture for predicting short-term equity trends by combining behavioral finance factors with traditional technical indicators. We employ transformer-based attention mechanisms to capture complex interactions between market sentiment, investor behavior, and price dynamics. Our model achieves state-of-the-art performance in forecasting daily returns across multiple equity markets, demonstrating practical applications for algorithmic trading systems.
