Maritime RAG

AI-powered system for maritime regulations and compliance assistance.

Project Overview

Maritime RAG is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation system designed to assist with international maritime law and SOLAS conventions. The system combines AI language models with a comprehensive knowledge base of maritime regulations to provide accurate, contextual answers to compliance questions.

Key Features

SOLAS Conventions

Comprehensive coverage of Safety of Life at Sea regulations and requirements.

RAG Architecture

Retrieval-Augmented Generation for accurate, source-backed responses.

Maritime Law

International maritime regulations and compliance frameworks.

Real-time Assistance

Query maritime regulations and receive immediate, contextual guidance.

Technology Stack

Python LangChain RAG Vector Database Maritime Regulations

Current Status

The project is currently in the proof-of-concept phase. Development focuses on building a robust knowledge base from SOLAS conventions and establishing reliable retrieval mechanisms for regulatory queries.
